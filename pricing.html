Frequently asked questions

Is this a one-time purchase or subscription?

One-time purchase. Pay once, use forever. Free updates are included for life. No recurring charges, no annual renewals.

What happens after the free trial?

The app locks and asks for a license key. Enter your key to unlock it instantly. No data is lost — your connections, registers, and settings are all preserved.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. Activation requires a one-time internet connection. After that, ModBus Pro works completely offline — perfect for field work on remote sites and air-gapped networks.

What if I need to move to a new computer?

Deactivate on your old machine from Settings, then activate on the new one. If you no longer have access to the old machine, contact support and we will reset your activation.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If ModBus Pro does not work for your use case, we will refund you in full.

Is there a free version?

The 7-day trial is full-featured with no restrictions. After that, a license is required. We believe in giving you the complete experience so you can make an informed decision.

What Modbus protocols are supported?

Modbus RTU (serial RS232/RS485) and Modbus TCP/IP. Function codes FC01 through FC06, FC15, FC16, FC22 (Mask Write), and FC23 (Read/Write Multiple).
